Modal Verbs

Modal verbs tell us how much something is possible or allowed.

can / could

I have ability

may / might

I have motivation / permission

shall / should

I have a strong reason to

must / have to

I am forced to

will / would

I have a strong intention

I can speak English.

I can not speak Vietnamese.

I may drive a car in Australia.

I may not drive a car in China.

I shall arrive at my exam on time.

I shall not be late to my exam.

(In China) I must drive a car on the right-hand side.

I must not rob a bank.

I will try my best at school.

I will not be lazy.

Can / May --- Could / Was Allowed To

Permission in the Past Tense

When we are talking about the past tense, we need to change the modal verb

present
I can (may) stay up late on weekends, but I can't (may not) stay up late on weekdays.
past
I could (was allowed) to stay up late on weekends, but I couldn't (wasn't allowed) stay up late on weekdays.

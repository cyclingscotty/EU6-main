I can (do something)(at some time), but I can't (do something)(at another time).

Can Vs May

Ability Vs Permission

Can I go to the amusement park instead of going to school?
May I go to the amusement park instead of going to school?
Yes, you probably can - you can take the bus; if you have enough money to buy the tickets: no problem.
Unless there is a very good reason, your parents and school will probably not give you permission.
So, while can is acceptable, may is better.
